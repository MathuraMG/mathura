Energy @ ITP

Interaction design, Programming

Tools Used : node.js, heroku, Inkscape
A set of UI tools that look at the energy consumption in the Interactive Telecommunication department at NYU.

Energy at ITP - Wall installation

This wall installation was a simple dashboard that explained the energy consumption on the department floor at any given instant as well as over the past week. The aim of this was to make people not only aware of the energy that was being consumed per room/per equipment, but also to humanize energy and make it fun and tangiable concept. To do so, we compared energy used in the building to energy in a potato or energy to use a hair dryer. This was displayed at the ITP Spring Show in 2016.

Energy at ITP - mobile

This mobile app looked at key rooms in ITP and informed users about the energy usage in each room/area.
